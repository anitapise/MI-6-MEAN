About Us

Jobox.in is the most innovative and second largest online job portal in India. over the past decade, Jobox.in has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

Jobox.in connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, Jobs.net focuses on the entire career growth of candidates.
