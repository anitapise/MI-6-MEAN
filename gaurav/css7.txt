Q 7.1 What is the difference between display:block and display:block-inline
Ans: The display:block can have  width,height and margin.The display:block-inline can have width,height,margin and padding.Compared to display: block , the major difference is that display: inline-block does not add a line-break after the element, so the element can sit next to other elements.
 
Q 7.2 What is the purpose of using ‘clear’ after floating elements?
Ans: This is an immensely powerful property, it helps bring our non-floated elements back into the normal flow, a behavior that we tend to expect by default.
