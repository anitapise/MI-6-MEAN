Abot us

JOBER DISK

jober disk.com is the most innovative and second largest online job portal in India. Founded in 2020, over the past decade,find job.com has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed 3.40 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on site

jober disk.com connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, Jober disk.com focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, Jober disk.com has launched Shine Learning - India’s largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses & certifications.

As the industry shifts towards mobile, Jober disk.com is leading the transition and is the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the Jober disk.com

jober disk.com works closely to bridge the gap between talent & opportunities and offers end-to-end recruitment solutions. Find Job Fair brings candidates and top employers under one roof. While Shine HR Conclave brings top HR leaders to share insights on latest trends, innovations & best practices in the HR industry. Jober disk.com also has a large reach through its print product, Jober disk – the Tuesday Job supplement of Hindustan Times– making it the only job portal with an integrated print and online offering
