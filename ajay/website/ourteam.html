George Brook

Founder

George Brook founded Ratio Capital in 2009 with an eye on building a unique culture for advisors and clients to have the most seamless and productive relationship possible. George has two children and enjoys basketball, golf and dreaming big.

Emely Hopson

Managing Partner

Emely Hopson joined Ratio Capital in 2019 after a career working as a financial advisor in the bank channel that included stays at Wachovia, Bank of the West, Citigroup and Union Bank. She left that world to pursue a more client friendly model that would allow her to have a more personal relationship with her clients. She is focused on working clients that value good advice and desire a long term goal-based relationship to get to their desired financial destination. Emely is married with three sons and enjoys golf, coaching little league and finding the positive side of things.
