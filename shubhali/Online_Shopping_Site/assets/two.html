        Top Notch Fashion with Ladies' Tops

        In the diverse world of women’s fashion, ladies’ designer tops occupy a prestigious place. The humble ladies' top has gone from being a bare essential to the epitome of style and elegance for the modern woman. Designer tops have evolved over the generations, blending in the old and adding onto them contemporary twists for a fashionable, up-to-date look. Today you don’t have to stick to straight tops or just printed tops worn over jeans or trousers. Myntra transports you to a style-jammed world of ladies’ fashion tops. Pick your favourite from an assortment of ladies' tops designed willfully to jazz up your feminine appeal. Why settle for mediocre ladies' tops when Myntra offers you the chance to look exceptional in ladies’ party tops at best prices.

        The advent of the sweltering heat calls for a prudent investment of vibrant ladies’ summer tops. These ladies’ cotton tops with vivacious prints beat the heat with your chilled-out ensemble of a white printed ladies’ short top with floral motifs over a pair of quintessential blue jeans. Reinvent your wardrobe with long tops and kaftan tops that not only elevate your appearance but also adds sophistication to it. Many women are unaware of the mighty strides the fashion industry has taken into the world of ladies’ top design. Here’s a profile of the variety of ladies' tops available:

        Timelessly Classic Tops

        1. The Bardot top, with its neck and shoulder-baring appeal, regales in the old-world charm. Metamorphosed from a classic ladies' top worn over trousers or a pencil skirt, to sitting prettily over denim skirts, shorts and ripped jeans. Avail of a plain Bardot top or experiment with a Bardot- crop top, ruffled top, lace top and scalloped top.
        2. Blouson Tops: The anytime-anywhere top for casual and formal events. Paired with the right accompaniment, it spells your mood like no other. A checked blouson top with white trousers gives you a chic look. A solid navy blue shirt-style blouson top with formal black trousers and a crisp dinner jacket could make you stand tall as a young, fashionable entrepreneur.
        3. Cinched waist top: Frantically waiting to showcase your well-toned abs then go daring with a crop-top, yet gotta be formal, then look to a cinched-waist top to do the magic.
        4. Go crazy with peplum tops, cold-shoulder tops and scalloped tops worn with the right accessories and dazzle your spectators.

        Charmingly Cute Tops

        1. Lace Tops: Arm yourself with a battalion of lace tops in white, solid colours and pastel shades to look cute for any occasion.
        2. Wrap Tops: These tops worn over long skirts easily give you a very boho-chic style. Pair them with trousers and solid pendants to highlight your beauty.
        3. Explore the range of layered tops, sheer tops, ruffle tops and printed tops to transform your style presence constantly.
